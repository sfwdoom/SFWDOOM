SFWDOOM: Plushie Edition 🪄💖

Welcome to the softest, most wholesome demon-booping experience ever created. SFWDOOM: Plushie Edition reimagines the classic fast-paced action of the 90s through a lens of cotton candy, marshmallow clouds, and magical friendship.

## The Story
You are the Plushie Protector. The Fuzzy Forest and its surrounding realms have been overrun by grumpy (but still very cute) entities. Armed with your Magic Sparkle Wand, you must journey through the clouds to boop the grumpiness away and restore peace to the Sugar Sanctuary.

## Key Features
 * Wholesome Combat: No "Doom," just "Boop!" Use your magic wand to send sparks and hearts at enemies.
 * Dynamic Movement: Features a "floaty" bobbing camera and sprint mechanics to glide through levels.
 * Ray-Casted Visuals: A custom-built pseudo-3D engine that renders a vibrant, cotton-candy world in real-time.
 * Combo System: Keep booping to build your combo meter and multiply your score!
 * Secret Portals: Discover hidden gateways like the one in Sugar Sanctuary to access secret challenge levels.
 * Global Leaderboard!: Compete for the highest score and become the ultimate Champion of Cheer.

## Controls
| Action | Control |
|---|---|
| Move | W A S D or On-Screen D-Pad |
| Boop (Attack) | Space or ✨ BOOP! Button |
| Sprint | Shift or 👟 Button |
| Turn | A / D keys |

> Note: When entering a new area, the player will automatically face the nearest enemy to keep the action flowing!

## Technical Details
The game is built using a custom Ray-Casting Engine written in vanilla JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas.

### Features:
 * Z-Buffering: Ensures sprites, projectiles, and particle effects render at the correct depth relative to the walls.
 * Collision Detection: Updated physics to prevent wall-clipping in dense areas like the Sugar Sanctuary.
 * Particle System: High-performance emoji-based particles for boops, confetti, and "ENTER" portals.
 * Responsive UI: Designed for both Desktop (Keyboard) and Mobile (Touch) play.
